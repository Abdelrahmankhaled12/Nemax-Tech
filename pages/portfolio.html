PORTFOLIO
We take pride in our portfolio of projects that highlight our expertise in Web3 technologies. Through our collaborations and contributions, we strive to push the boundaries of what is possible in the decentralized ecosystem. Each project represents our commitment to excellence, innovation, and the advancement of Web3 technologies.

Graphtronauts

Graphtronauts is one of our flagship projects, demonstrating our expertise in building thriving Web3 communities. As the largest non-official community for long-term holders and delegators of The Graph network, Graphtronauts has established itself as a vibrant hub for knowledge-sharing, collaboration, and community engagement. Through our strategic community-building efforts, we have fostered a strong and passionate network of individuals who are dedicated to the success and growth of The Graph ecosystem. Join us on this exciting journey as we explore the limitless possibilities of decentralized indexing and querying.

Axie Infinity Validator for Ronin Chain

We are proud to have served as a validator for the Ronin Chain of the popular blockchain-based game, Axie Infinity. As a validator, we played a crucial role in maintaining the security, reliability, and integrity of the Ronin Chain. By validating transactions and participating in network consensus, we ensured that players could enjoy seamless gameplay experiences while leveraging the power of blockchain technology. Our commitment to maintaining a robust and efficient validator node contributed to the success and scalability of the Ronin Chain.

Running an Indexer Node for The Graph Network

Another notable project in our portfolio is our involvement in running an indexer node for The Graph network. As an indexer, we have played an integral role in providing reliable and accurate data indexing services to the decentralized web. By maintaining an efficient and resilient indexer node, we have contributed to the accessibility and usability of decentralized applications built on The Graph. Our dedication to data integrity and efficient indexing has enabled developers to create innovative solutions that leverage the power of decentralized data.

Running Nodes for Presearch

We are also actively involved in running nodes for Presearch, a decentralized search engine powered by blockchain technology. As nodes operators, we contribute to the stability and reliability of the Presearch network. By hosting and maintaining nodes, we support the decentralized infrastructure that allows users to search the web with enhanced privacy and control. Our dedication to running efficient nodes ensures that users can access Presearch's search capabilities seamlessly while enjoying the benefits of decentralized search.
